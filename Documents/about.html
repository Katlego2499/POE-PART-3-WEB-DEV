Who we are

Hey, I'm Katlego. It all started in my grandma's attic, buried under a stack of old CDs—a perfectly faded, vintage Coogi sweater. That one find sparked an obsession with the unique style and quality of past eras. I started thrifting not just for myself, but for the thrill of the hunt.

Kats thrift store was born from a simple idea: what if I could do the digging for you? I spend my weekends searching for those perfect, one-of-a-kind streetwear grails and iconic Y2K pieces so you can build a wardrobe that truly stands out. We're all about sustainable style, self-expression, and of course, that killer fit.

Our mission and vision

Our mission is to make sustainable fashion accessible, fun, and fresh for everyone. We envision a world where style meets conscious choices, and every outfit tells a story.

Why thrifting matters

By choosing thrifted and vintage pieces, you're helping reduce waste, lower fashion's environmental footprint, and keep timeless style alive.
